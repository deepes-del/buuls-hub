Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What are the course timings?

We offer flexible batch timings including morning, afternoon, evening, and weekend batches to suit working professionals and students.

❓ Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both offline and online classes. You can choose the mode that suits you best.

❓ What is the course duration?

Zero to Hero: 2-3 weeks. Hero to Legend: 6-10 weeks. Duration varies based on batch schedule and your learning pace.

❓ Do I need prior knowledge?

For Zero to Hero: No prior knowledge required. For Hero to Legend: Basic market knowledge is recommended.

❓ What is the fee structure?

Zero to Hero: ₹6,000. Hero to Legend: ₹35,000. Payment plans available. Contact us for details.

❓ Is there ongoing support?

Yes! All students get lifetime WhatsApp support and access to our community of traders.
